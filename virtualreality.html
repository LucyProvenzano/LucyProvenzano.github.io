Virtual Reality

At the beginning of the coronavirus pandemic the artist turned to virtual reality in order to display works and continue her public practice. After a while this vehicle for exhibition became an artwork in itself and she started to use virtual reality to create immersive spaces and experiences. It is now one of her main mediums for creating artwork. For now, all of her virtual reality progress videos and final walkthroughs can be viewed on her Youtube Channel until they are uploaded online for public access and viewing.
