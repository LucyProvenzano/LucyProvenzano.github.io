Eggman

The 'Eggman' project began as a series of sketches and idea for an egg shaped character living in the modern world. His past as a child actor and ex-superstar had left him rich, and the project follows his life in london. Eggman has both an Instagram Account and a YouTube channel Youtube Channel where his fans can watch as he doccuments his lavish lifestyle.
